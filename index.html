Tic Tac Toe: Battle of 'The Barn'

Instructions

There's a battle brewing in the Barn! Which Housewife of Potomac are you choosing in this epic feud? Take the first turn to play as Monique Samuels and take the second turn to play as Candiace Dillard Bassett in this showdown Tic Tac Toe game. May the best housewife win!
